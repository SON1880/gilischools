About Us

Introduction

GILI Schools is a group of private educational institutions located in Kibaha and Singida, Tanzania. We are dedicated to offering high-quality education from Baby Class to Advanced Level (A-Level), grounded in strong academic performance, moral values, and student leadership.

Our schools include: GILI Pre & Primary Schools in Kibaha and Singida, GILI Secondary Schools (Girls & Boys boarding) in Kibaha, and the GILI Mixed Secondary School in Singida. Each campus offers a structured, safe, and inspiring learning environment for boys and girls.
